About Lethal Company

Lethal Company is a cooperative horror game where players work together to survive terrifying monsters and complete objectives. This guide provides detailed information about the game's monsters, mechanics, and strategies.

Monsters

Monster 1: The Shadow Stalker

Behavior: The Shadow Stalker is a stealthy predator that lurks in dark areas. It attacks when players are isolated.

Weakness: Bright light can temporarily stun it.

Monster 2: The Blood Crawler

Behavior: This monster crawls on walls and ceilings, dropping down to ambush players.

Weakness: Loud noises can disorient it.

Monster 3: The Howler

Behavior: The Howler emits a deafening scream that attracts other monsters.

Weakness: It has low health and can be killed quickly if caught off guard.

Game Mechanics

Objectives

Players must work together to collect resources, complete tasks, and survive until extraction.

Teamwork

Communication and coordination are key to surviving the horrors of Lethal Company.

Extraction

Once objectives are completed, players must reach the extraction point before time runs out.
