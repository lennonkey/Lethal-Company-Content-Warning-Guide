About Lethal Company

Lethal Company is a cooperative horror game where players work together to survive terrifying monsters and complete objectives. This guide provides detailed information about the game's monsters, items, maps, and more.

Monsters

The Shadow Stalker

Behavior: A stealthy predator that lurks in dark areas. It attacks isolated players.

Weakness: Bright light stuns it temporarily.

The Blood Crawler

Behavior: Crawls on walls and ceilings, ambushing players from above.

Weakness: Loud noises disorient it.

The Howler

Behavior: Emits a deafening scream that attracts other monsters.

Weakness: Low health; can be killed quickly.

The Swarm

Behavior: A group of small, fast creatures that overwhelm players.

Weakness: Area-of-effect weapons like the Flamethrower.

The Behemoth

Behavior: A massive, slow-moving monster with high health.

Weakness: Focus fire on its weak spots.

Items

Flamethrower

Description: Effective against swarms of smaller monsters.

Usage: Short-range, high damage.

Shotgun

Description: Powerful close-range weapon.

Usage: Best for taking down large monsters quickly.

Stun Gun

Description: Temporarily immobilizes monsters.

Usage: Useful for escaping dangerous situations.

Medkit

Description: Restores health.

Usage: Use when low on health to survive longer.

Flare

Description: Provides temporary light in dark areas.

Usage: Use to scare off light-sensitive monsters.

Maps

Abandoned Facility

Description: A dark, maze-like facility with tight corridors and hidden rooms.

Tips: Use flares to mark your path and avoid getting lost.

Forest Outpost

Description: An outdoor map with dense foliage and limited visibility.

Tips: Stick to open areas to avoid ambushes.

Underground Bunker

Description: A claustrophobic underground map with narrow tunnels.

Tips: Watch for traps and hidden monsters.

Industrial Complex

Description: A large, multi-level map with machinery and hazards.

Tips: Use verticality to your advantage.

Deserted Ship

Description: A derelict spaceship with zero-gravity areas and malfunctioning systems.

Tips: Use magnetic boots to navigate zero-gravity zones.

Frozen Wasteland

Description: A snowy, open map with blizzards and freezing temperatures.

Tips: Stay near heat sources to avoid freezing.
