About Lethal Company

Lethal Company is a cooperative horror survival game where players work as employees of a mysterious corporation. Your mission is to scavenge abandoned moons and facilities for valuable resources while avoiding deadly monsters and environmental hazards. Teamwork is essential as you navigate dark, eerie environments, manage limited resources, and complete objectives to meet your profit quota. With procedurally generated maps, unpredictable enemies, and a tense atmosphere, Lethal Company offers a thrilling and replayable experience for horror and survival game enthusiasts.

How to Play Lethal Company

Objective: Work as a team to scavenge resources from abandoned moons and facilities while avoiding deadly monsters and environmental hazards. Meet your profit quota to survive and progress.

Key Mechanics:

  • Teamwork: Coordinate with your team to divide tasks, such as scavenging, defending, and managing resources.
  • Resource Management: Collect valuable items and manage your inventory carefully to maximize efficiency.
  • Combat: Use weapons and tools to defend yourself against monsters. Some enemies require specific strategies to defeat.
  • Exploration: Navigate procedurally generated maps to find loot and complete objectives.
  • Profit Quota: Meet the quota set by the company to avoid penalties and progress to more challenging missions.

About Content Warning

Content Warning is a thrilling multiplayer horror game where players work together to investigate and document paranormal activities. Armed with cameras and limited equipment, you and your team must explore haunted locations, capture evidence of supernatural phenomena, and survive terrifying encounters. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and quick thinking as you face unpredictable threats and uncover the mysteries behind each location. With procedurally generated events and a dynamic scare system, Content Warning delivers a unique and spine-chilling experience every time.

How to Play Content Warning

Objective: Work as a team to investigate haunted locations, capture evidence of supernatural phenomena, and survive terrifying encounters. Your goal is to document as much evidence as possible while avoiding deadly entities and environmental hazards.

Key Mechanics:

  • Teamwork: Coordinate with your team to divide tasks, such as filming, defending, and managing equipment.
  • Evidence Collection: Use cameras and other equipment to capture paranormal activity. The more evidence you collect, the higher your score.
  • Survival: Avoid or defend against hostile entities. Some entities require specific strategies to evade or defeat.
  • Exploration: Navigate procedurally generated maps to find evidence and complete objectives.
  • Resource Management: Manage your equipment and resources carefully to maximize efficiency and survival.

Content Warning Entities

Shadow Figure

Behavior: A dark, humanoid figure that lurks in the shadows. It is highly aggressive and will chase players if provoked.

Weakness: Can be temporarily repelled with a flashlight.

Wraith

Behavior: A ghostly entity that can phase through walls and objects. It is attracted to sound and will hunt players who make too much noise.

Weakness: Can be temporarily stunned with a stun grenade.

Poltergeist

Behavior: A mischievous entity that manipulates objects and creates environmental hazards. It is not directly aggressive but can cause significant harm.

Weakness: Can be temporarily banished with a spirit box.

Banshee

Behavior: A female spirit that emits a piercing scream. The scream can disorient and damage players if they are too close.

Weakness: Can be temporarily silenced with a soundproof barrier.

Demon

Behavior: A powerful and aggressive entity that can possess players and objects. It is highly dangerous and requires teamwork to defeat.

Weakness: Can be exorcised with a crucifix and holy water.
