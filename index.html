About Lethal Company

Overview: Lethal Company is a cooperative horror game where players take on the role of scavengers exploring abandoned industrial facilities. The goal is to collect scrap, avoid deadly monsters, and escape before time runs out.

Key Features:

  • Cooperative multiplayer experience.
  • Procedurally generated levels for replayability.
  • Terrifying creatures that react to player actions.
  • Limited resources and communication tools.

How to Play Lethal Company

Objective: Work as a team to scavenge abandoned facilities for valuable scrap while avoiding deadly monsters and hazards.

Key Mechanics:

  • Teamwork: Coordinate tasks such as scavenging, defending, and navigating through dark environments.
  • Resource Management: Collect and manage scrap and tools efficiently to meet your profit quota.
  • Stealth & Combat: Avoid or fight monsters using available items, each of which offers unique advantages.
  • Procedural Levels: Each playthrough is different due to randomly generated maps and enemy placements.

About Content Warning

Overview: Content Warning is a paranormal investigation game where players document supernatural encounters using specialized equipment. The goal is to capture as much paranormal activity on camera as possible while surviving the investigation.

Key Features:

  • Immersive horror experience with realistic audio effects.
  • Multiple haunted locations with unique supernatural threats.
  • Cooperative and competitive gameplay mechanics.
  • Equipment upgrades and unlockable tools for better investigations.

How to Play Content Warning

Objective: Work as a team to investigate haunted locations, capture evidence of supernatural activity, and survive the horrors within.

Key Mechanics:

  • Teamwork: Communicate effectively with your team to ensure everyone's safety.
  • Equipment Usage: Use cameras, motion detectors, and other tools to document paranormal events.
  • Survival: Avoid supernatural threats and manage your fear levels.
  • Investigation: Gather and analyze evidence to determine the nature of the haunting.
