About Lethal Company

Lethal Company is a cooperative horror survival game where players take on the role of employees working for a mysterious corporation. Your mission is to scavenge abandoned moons and facilities for valuable resources while avoiding deadly monsters and environmental hazards. Teamwork is essential as you navigate dark, eerie environments, manage limited resources, and complete objectives to meet your profit quota. With procedurally generated maps, unpredictable enemies, and a tense atmosphere, Lethal Company offers a thrilling and replayable experience for horror and survival game enthusiasts.

Lethal Company Monsters

The Shadow Stalker

Behavior: A stealthy predator that lurks in dark areas. It attacks isolated players.

Weakness: Bright light stuns it temporarily.

The Blood Crawler

Behavior: Crawls on walls and ceilings, ambushing players from above.

Weakness: Loud noises disorient it.

The Howler

Behavior: Emits a deafening scream that attracts other monsters.

Weakness: Low health; can be killed quickly.

The Swarm

Behavior: A group of small, fast creatures that overwhelm players.

Weakness: Area-of-effect weapons like the Flamethrower.

The Behemoth

Behavior: A massive, slow-moving monster with high health.

Weakness: Focus fire on its weak spots.

About Content Warning

Content Warning is a thrilling multiplayer horror game where players work together to investigate and document paranormal activities. Armed with cameras and limited equipment, you and your team must explore haunted locations, capture evidence of supernatural phenomena, and survive terrifying encounters. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and quick thinking as you face unpredictable threats and uncover the mysteries behind each location. With procedurally generated events and a dynamic scare system, Content Warning delivers a unique and spine-chilling experience every time.

Content Warning Entities

Ghosts

Description: Spirits that haunt specific areas and can manifest in various forms.

Behavior: They may follow players, create noises, or attack when provoked.

Demons

Description: Powerful entities that require specific rituals to banish.

Behavior: Highly aggressive and can possess players or objects.

Poltergeists

Description: Mischievous spirits that manipulate objects in the environment.

Behavior: They throw items, slam doors, and create chaos.

Shadows

Description: Dark, humanoid figures that stalk players.

Behavior: They move quickly and attack when players are isolated.

Wraiths

Description: Invisible entities that emit eerie sounds.

Behavior: They drain sanity and can only be detected with specialized equipment.

Revenants

Description: Vengeful spirits tied to specific locations.

Behavior: They become more aggressive as players stay longer in their territory.
