About Lethal Company

Lethal Company is a cooperative horror survival game where players take on the role of employees working for a mysterious corporation. Your mission is to scavenge abandoned moons and facilities for valuable resources while avoiding deadly monsters and environmental hazards. Teamwork is essential as you navigate dark, eerie environments, manage limited resources, and complete objectives to meet your profit quota. With procedurally generated maps, unpredictable enemies, and a tense atmosphere, Lethal Company offers a thrilling and replayable experience for horror and survival game enthusiasts.

How to Play Lethal Company

Objective: Work as a team to scavenge resources from abandoned moons and facilities while avoiding deadly monsters and environmental hazards. Meet your profit quota to survive and progress.

Key Mechanics:

  • Teamwork: Coordinate with your team to divide tasks, such as scavenging, defending, and managing resources.
  • Resource Management: Collect valuable items and manage your inventory carefully to maximize efficiency.
  • Combat: Use weapons and tools to defend yourself against monsters. Some enemies require specific strategies to defeat.
  • Exploration: Navigate procedurally generated maps to find loot and complete objectives.
  • Profit Quota: Meet the quota set by the company to avoid penalties and progress to more challenging missions.

About Content Warning

Content Warning is a thrilling multiplayer horror game where players work together to investigate and document paranormal activities. Armed with cameras and limited equipment, you and your team must explore haunted locations, capture evidence of supernatural phenomena, and survive terrifying encounters. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and quick thinking as you face unpredictable threats and uncover the mysteries behind each location. With procedurally generated events and a dynamic scare system, Content Warning delivers a unique and spine-chilling experience every time.

How to Play Content Warning

Objective: Work as a team to investigate haunted locations, capture evidence of paranormal activity, and survive terrifying encounters.

Key Mechanics:

  • Teamwork: Coordinate with your team to explore, document, and survive.
  • Documentation: Use cameras and other equipment to capture evidence of paranormal activity.
  • Survival: Avoid or outsmart hostile entities while managing your sanity and resources.
  • Exploration: Investigate procedurally generated locations to uncover mysteries and complete objectives.
  • Dynamic Events: Face unpredictable threats and challenges that change with each playthrough.
